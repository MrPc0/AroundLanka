7-Day Adventure Package

From golden beaches to misty mountains – an unforgettable week in Sri Lanka.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

🌍 Day 1 – Arrival & Negombo

✈️ Morning

  • Arrive Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB)
  • Private transfer to Negombo (Book PickMe)

🏨 Mid-Morning

  • Check-in: Heritance Negombo

🏖️ Afternoon

  • Swim & relax at Negombo Beach
  • Stroll the Dutch Canal

🍲 Evening

  • Seafood dinner at Lords Restaurant
🏯 Day 2 – Sigiriya & Dambulla

🚗 Early Morning

  • Drive to Sigiriya (~3.5 hrs) Book Transfer

🏰 Late Morning

  • Climb Sigiriya Rock Fortress

🕍 Afternoon

  • Visit Dambulla Cave Temple

🌅 Evening

  • Sunset at Pidurangala Rock
  • Overnight: Cinnamon Lodge Habarana
🐘 Day 3 – Minneriya Safari & Kandy

🌿 Morning

  • Jeep Safari at Minneriya National Park

🚗 Midday Transfer

  • Drive to Kandy (~3 hrs)

🎶 Evening

  • Witness traditional Kandyan dance
  • Visit Temple of the Tooth
🚂 Day 4 – Kandy to Nuwara Eliya

🚆 Morning

  • Scenic train ride Kandy ➜ Nanu Oya (book seats early)

🍃 Afternoon

  • Visit Blue Field Tea Factory
  • Stroll around Gregory Lake

🛌 Evening

  • Overnight at Jetwing St. Andrew’s
🥾 Day 5 – Horton Plains & Ella

🌄 Early Morning

  • Hike Horton Plains / World’s End

🚆 Midday

  • Train ride to Ella – famed for 9-Arch Bridge views Ella train ride

🏡 Evening

  • Overnight: 98 Acres Resort
🏝️ Day 6 – Yala National Park

🐆 Morning Safari

  • Jeep safari to spot leopards & elephants at Yala NP

🏖️ Afternoon

  • Relax at nearby Kirinda Beach Kirinda beach

🌙 Night

  • Stay at Jetwing Yala
🌊 Day 7 – Galle & Departure

☕ Morning

  • Drive to Galle (~3.5 hrs) Transfering Ways

🏰 Midday

  • Relax at Jungle beach
  • Lunch at a The hideout Restaurant Hideout Restaurant

✈️ Evening

  • Sunset at Galle Fort
  • Transfer to Colombo Airport for departure Transfering Ways
